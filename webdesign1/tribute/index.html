Sea Turtles

Sea Turtles are a graceful reptiles. They are also known as marine turtles. They take their time and move through the water to meet their goals. Sea tutles come in all shapes, sies, and colors. The majority of a sea turtle's body is protected by its two part shell. The shell is made up of smaller plates called scutes.

  • Loggerhead Sea Turtle
  • Kemp's ridley sea turtle
